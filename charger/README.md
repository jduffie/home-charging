# charger

Device-specific configuration for **your** smart charger lives here once it's worked
out: pairing, app/account setup, any home-automation or load-management hookup, and
the knobs unique to your hardware. This folder owns only what is concrete and
specific to your device — not general advice. Situational sizing/install questions
("what amperage?", "do I need load management?", "hardwired vs plug-in?") are
**not** answered in prose here; they depend on your home and panel — use the
copy-paste prompt instead:
[../prompts/charger-sizing.md](../prompts/charger-sizing.md).
